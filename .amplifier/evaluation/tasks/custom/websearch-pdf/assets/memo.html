Halcyon Systems, Inc. — Operations Committee

MEMORANDUM

To: Program Leads, Finance, and Communications
From: Office of the Chief Operating Officer
Date: 14 January 2031
Re: FY2031 Flagship Program Designation

Following the December strategic review, the Operations Committee has designated Project Borealis-4 (Propulsion Systems) as the company's flagship program for fiscal year 2031. Flagship status reflects the program's strategic priority for the year and guides external messaging, executive reporting cadence, and cross-team support commitments.

Program leads should align their FY2031 milestone reviews and quarterly updates accordingly. Questions may be directed to the Office of the COO.
